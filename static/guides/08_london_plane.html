London Plane Tree

Scientific Name: Platanus × acerifolia

Type: Deciduous Broadleaf

Average Lifespan: 100–200 years

Native Region: Urban areas worldwide

Care & Maintenance

  • Watering: Moderate, deep watering preferred
  • Soil: Adaptable, prefers loamy
  • Sunlight: Full sun
  • Pruning: Winter

Environmental Benefits

  • Handles air pollution well
  • Fast-growing shade tree
  • Beautiful peeling bark

Growth Timeline

  • Years 1–5: Quick vertical growth
  • Years 6–15: Can reach 20m
  • Year 15+: Up to 30m or more

Fun Fact: Commonly planted along Vancouver streets.
