Bigleaf Maple

Scientific Name: Acer macrophyllum

Type: Deciduous Broadleaf

Average Lifespan: 100–300 years

Native Region: Pacific Northwest

Care & Maintenance

  • Watering: Moderate during dry spells
  • Soil: Moist, fertile
  • Sunlight: Prefers full sun
  • Pruning: Late summer or fall

Environmental Benefits

  • Provides food for pollinators
  • Leaves decompose to enrich soil
  • Provides shade and beauty

Growth Timeline

  • Years 1–3: Root development
  • Years 4–15: Rapid growth
  • Year 15+: Can grow 15–30m tall

Fun Fact: Its leaves can grow up to 12 inches wide!
