Horse Chestnut

Scientific Name: Aesculus hippocastanum

Type: Deciduous Broadleaf

Average Lifespan: 200–300 years

Native Region: Europe (introduced)

Care & Maintenance

  • Watering: Weekly in dry periods
  • Soil: Moist, fertile
  • Sunlight: Full sun
  • Pruning: Late winter

Environmental Benefits

  • Large flowers attract pollinators
  • Provides dense shade
  • Iconic conker fruits

Growth Timeline

  • Years 1–5: Initial growth
  • Years 6–20: Rapid growth phase
  • Year 20+: Can exceed 25m

Fun Fact: The seeds are toxic if eaten raw!
