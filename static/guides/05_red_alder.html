Red Alder

Scientific Name: Alnus rubra

Type: Deciduous Broadleaf

Average Lifespan: 40–60 years

Native Region: Coastal BC and Pacific Northwest

Care & Maintenance

  • Watering: Needs consistent moisture
  • Soil: Moist, well-drained
  • Sunlight: Full sun
  • Pruning: As needed for shape

Environmental Benefits

  • Fixes nitrogen in soil
  • Improves forest regeneration
  • Supports stream ecosystems

Growth Timeline

  • Years 1–3: Quick sprouting
  • Years 4–20: Steady vertical growth
  • Year 20+: Can reach 25m

Fun Fact: One of the fastest-growing native trees in BC!
