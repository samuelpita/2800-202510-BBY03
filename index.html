What is Lorax?

Lorax is a tree adoption and care app that can be used for education or as a plant care tool.

Using a map of marked trees, you can select different ones and view their species, average water needs, and more.

Once you've adopted a tree, you can view it's stats, get watering reminders, and upload a photo every day to create a growth timeline!
