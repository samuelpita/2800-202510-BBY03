Norway Maple

Scientific Name: Acer platanoides

Type: Deciduous Broadleaf

Average Lifespan: 60–100 years

Native Region: Europe (introduced to BC)

Care & Maintenance

  • Watering: Occasional once established
  • Soil: Tolerant of many types
  • Sunlight: Full sun to partial shade
  • Pruning: Winter or early spring

Environmental Benefits

  • Urban shade tree
  • Tolerates pollution
  • Bright yellow fall foliage

Growth Timeline

  • Years 1–5: Fast initial growth
  • Years 6–20: Can grow up to 15m
  • Year 20+: Spreads wide canopy

Fun Fact: Can outcompete native trees if not managed.
