Japanese Maple

Scientific Name: Acer palmatum

Type: Deciduous Ornamental

Average Lifespan: 50–100 years

Native Region: East Asia, widely planted in BC

Care & Maintenance

  • Watering: Weekly or more during heat
  • Soil: Well-drained, slightly acidic
  • Sunlight: Partial shade
  • Pruning: Late fall or winter

Environmental Benefits

  • Beautiful fall colors
  • Compact and suitable for gardens
  • Provides aesthetic value

Growth Timeline

  • Years 1–3: Shallow root growth
  • Years 4–10: Slow steady growth
  • Year 10+: Grows 4–8m tall

Fun Fact: Comes in hundreds of cultivated varieties.
