Cherry Blossom Trees

Scientific Name: Prunus spp.

Type: Deciduous Flowering Tree

Average Lifespan: 20–40 years

Native Region: Urban Vancouver and East Asia

Care & Maintenance

  • Watering: Weekly when dry
  • Soil: Well-drained, fertile
  • Sunlight: Full sun
  • Pruning: After flowering

Environmental Benefits

  • Beautiful spring blossoms
  • Attracts pollinators
  • Cultural symbol of renewal

Growth Timeline

  • Years 1–2: Establishment
  • Years 3–10: Blossom maturity
  • Year 10+: Reaches full height of 6–12m

Fun Fact: Vancouver is home to over 40,000 cherry blossom trees!
