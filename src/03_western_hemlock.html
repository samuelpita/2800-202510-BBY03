Western Hemlock

Scientific Name: Tsuga heterophylla

Type: Evergreen Conifer

Average Lifespan: 400–500 years

Native Region: Pacific Northwest forests

Care & Maintenance

  • Watering: Moderate, keep soil moist
  • Soil: Rich, acidic, well-drained
  • Sunlight: Partial to full shade
  • Pruning: Minimal; shape in late winter

Environmental Benefits

  • Provides shade and moisture for understory plants
  • Supports biodiversity
  • Improves soil quality

Growth Timeline

  • Years 1–5: Slow growth
  • Years 6–20: Steady increase in height
  • Year 20+: Mature height of 30–50m

Fun Fact: It’s the provincial tree of British Columbia!
